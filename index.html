Ise Grand Shrine

Ise Grand Shrine, officially called Ise Jingu, is a shrine dedicated to the celestial sun goddess Amaterasu(Amaterasu-Omikami). The area is roughly the same size as the center of Paris. More than 1,500 rituals are preformed yearly for the Imperial family, for world peace and the hugh harvest.
